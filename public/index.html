Understanding what data is sent to AI and how it's used

What Data is Sent to AI?

When you ask questions to the AI assistant or use the Food Safety Checker:

  • Your Question/Query: The text you type is sent to AI API (DeepSeek or OpenAI)
  • Conversation Context: Previous messages in the same chat session (for continuity)
  • NOT Sent: Your name, email, phone number, pet photos, or other personal data

AI Service Providers

We use these AI providers:

  • DeepSeek AI: Primary AI for emergency questions and food safety
  • OpenAI (ChatGPT): Alternative AI provider

Both providers:

  • Process your questions on their servers
  • May use data to improve their AI models (check their privacy policies)
  • Are bound by their own data protection policies

Location Data Usage

We only access your location when you explicitly request it:

  • Tap "Find Nearby Vets" button
  • Permission prompt appears (you can deny)
  • If granted, we get your approximate location
  • Location is used to open Maps app with nearby veterinary clinics
  • Location is NOT stored or sent to our servers

AdMob Data Collection

For free users, Google AdMob collects:

  • Device type, OS version
  • Ad impressions and clicks
  • Approximate location (for relevant ads)
  • Anonymous advertising ID

Premium users see NO ads and AdMob does not collect their data.

Data Retention

  • Pet Profiles: Stored until you delete them

Data Security

  • Firebase security rules prevent unauthorized access

  • Regular security audits
  • No data sold to third parties
